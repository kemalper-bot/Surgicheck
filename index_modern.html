About the Platform

What is SurgiCheck?

A rule-based, traceable clinical governance platform built for surgical workflows.

The Core Problem It Solves

In aesthetic and plastic surgery, patient clearance decisions are often inconsistent, undocumented, and difficult to audit. Different clinicians apply different criteria, creating liability exposure and operational inefficiency. SurgiCheck standardizes this process through deterministic rule logic â€” ensuring every patient assessment follows the same documented criteria, every time.

The Solution Approach

Unlike AI-based systems, SurgiCheck uses transparent, clinician-defined rules to generate risk signals and clearance recommendations. This deterministic approach guarantees explainability and auditability â€” critical in regulated healthcare environments. The platform produces structured Cleared / Conditional / Denied classifications with full documentation trails, not black-box predictions.
